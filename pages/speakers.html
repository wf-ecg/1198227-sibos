Mobile banking

New technologies that improve the customer experience
Tuesday, September 17
4:00 pm to 5:00 pm
Conference room 3 - CR3

Featuring:
Mike Kennedy, head of Innovation and Payments Strategy View bio (PDF*)

How to generate value and develop an infrastructure that supports the strategic use of gamification and social media to improve the customer experience.

 

Risk management

From grapple to grip
Wednesday, September 18
2:00 pm to 3:00 pm
Conference room 1 - CR1

Featuring:
Tom Lickiss, head of Business Risk for GFI View bio (PDF*)

Banks are exposed to a wide and increasing range of risks that can have an impact on their reputation. The panel will discuss how banks apply a risk-based approach to measure and mitigate their liabilities. How do they move from understanding risk to applying controls in line with their risk appetite? How can they benchmark their policies and procedures with global best practices?
