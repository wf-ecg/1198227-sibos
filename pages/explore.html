Explore Dubai

    

Located on the southern shore of the Arabian Gulf, Dubai is the second-largest of the seven Emirates that make up the United Arab Emirates. This thriving metropolis of over two million residents features architecture and attractions that rival the best that the rest of the world has to offer.

While in Dubai, you may want to visit:

    
  • Burj Khalifa the world’s tallest building, features a 124th-floor outdoor observation deck with a telescope that allows you to examine the landscape in real-time or view previously saved images from different times of day and weather conditions.
  • Dolphin Bay one of the world’s largest dolphin habitats gives you a chance to stand, swim, or scuba dive with these beautiful, intelligent creatures.
  • Ski Dubai is the world’s third-largest indoor ski slope and the first to open in the Middle East.
  • Dubai Fountain shoots water up to 500 feet high as music plays during daily performances on the Burj Khalifa Lake.
  • Dubai Aquarium & Underwater Zoo one of the largest tanks in the world, is home to more than 33,000 animals and sea creatures you can examine from inside its acrylic walk-through tunnel.
